Three Things

Before using Three Things, you should understand where it keeps your data.

Three Things is a 100% client-side browser-based app; it transfers no data to any servers.

This means that all your data — all the things you enter — it’s all saved in your Web browser.

So if your browser gets reset, or if your computer is lost, then all your data is gone.

An export feature will be implemented soon, but in the meantime proceed with caution!
